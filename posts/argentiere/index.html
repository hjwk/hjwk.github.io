Intro#

Getting there and back#

Car through France.

Day hikes#

The program’s name is “5 summits: on to the 3K” so each day is supposed to be a hike to the top of a mountain. We did:

  • Aiguillette des Possettes (warm-up day)
  • Refuge Albert 1er
  • La jonction
  • Le Buet
